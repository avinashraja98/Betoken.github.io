WHITE PAPER FORMAL ANALYSIS

The control over the fund's assets is denominated by Kairo tokens. During every investment cycle, each participant buys assets for the fund by staking Kairo proportional to the size of the purchase. (e.g. 'Stake 10 Kairo to buy 10 OMG tokens')

After they sell an asset they invested in for the fund, the staker wins Kairo tokens proportional to the ROI (return on investment), or lose Kairo if the ROI was negative.

Over time, the control over fund assets flows to the best investors, a win-win for both the performance of the fund as well as the managers. Moreover, a proportion of profits is distributed to Kairo token holders every month, providing additional value to the token.

Everything Runs on Smart Contracts

The entire Betoken fund is operated by a set of smart conrtracts on the Ethereum blockchain. Immutable and transparent code & parameters means you don't need to worry about hidden clauses, extra fees, or any other fishy business. We hide nothing from you.

Investment decisions are automatically carried out using Kyber Network, a decentralized exchange for ERC20 tokens. They don't charge any fees, a great plus for our investors. They operate solely on smart contracts as well, so the exchange process is also transparent.
