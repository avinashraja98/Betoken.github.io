Great performance guaranteed by prediction market

The Betoken hedge fund is managed not by private fund managers with opaque investment strategies, but by an open and transparent prediction market where people bet on performances of crypto assets. Prediction markets have been demonstrated to be unreasonably effective at predicting future outcomes, and Betoken harnesses its power to make investment decisions.

âšª

A meritocracy where forecasters race to profit the fund

The control over fund assets is denominated by Kairo tokens. During every investment cycle, each participant stakes Kairo tokens to make investment proposals for the fund (ex. buy 10 BAT tokens), which are subsequently executed. If a proposal is proved profitable at the end of the investment cycle, those who staked in support of it wins the Kairo tokens staked by the opposing side, and vise versa. Over time, the control over fund assets flows to the best forecasters, ensuring performance of the fund. Moreover, forecasters are rewarded every cycle with ETH proportional to their Kairo balances, incentivizing them to make accurate predictions.

Lowering barrier of entry

Anyone can choose to invest in the fund, make investment proposals, and win Kairo, all you need is an Ethereum account. There's no minimum investment, no need for invitations, and no wealth requirements. This allows Betoken to tap into capital that hedge funds can't traditionally utilize.

Machine learning support

We expect many forecasters to use machine learning powered scripts to automatically make investment proposals, and we plan to make it easier for them by making a Python API for interacting with Betoken. This allows forecasters to use powerful ML packages like TensorFlow, Theano, and Keras to make predictions for the fund.

Completely automated

All funds are stored in smart contracts - eliminating counterparty risk and allowing fast, automated payments to winning forecasters. Investment proposals are automatically turned into trade orders and placed onto decentralized exchanges. Depositing and withdrawing funds are also completely automated using the Ethereum network.
